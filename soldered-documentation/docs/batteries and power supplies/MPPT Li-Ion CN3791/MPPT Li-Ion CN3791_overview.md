## MPPT Li-Ion CN3791 charger board

The **MPPT Li-Ion CN3791 charger board** charges Li-Ion batteries from a solar panel. The onboard **CN3791** PWM switch-mode controller handles charging in constant current and constant voltage modes. It connects to the battery via a JST connector and to the solar panel via KF235 screw terminals.

## Key features

- **Input Voltage:** 4.5V - 28V
- **Charge Current:** up to 4A (programmable)
- **Constant Voltage:** 4.2V ± 1%
- **Switching Frequency:** 300 kHz
- **Battery overvoltage protection**
- **IC:** CN3791
- **Dimensions:** 54 x 38 mm (2.1 x 1.5 inches)
