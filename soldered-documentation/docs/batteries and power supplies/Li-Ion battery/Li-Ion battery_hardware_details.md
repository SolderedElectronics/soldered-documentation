The **Li-Ion batteries** are rated for **1C discharge** (fully charged or discharged in one hour), suitable for low to moderate power use. Each includes onboard protection against overcharging, over-discharging, and short circuits, and uses a JST-PH-2mm connector.

<WarningBox>The wiring polarity on these batteries is opposite to some other products using the same JST 2-pin connector. Red is positive (+), black is negative (-). Check polarity before connecting.</WarningBox>

<InfoBox>The battery polarity and connector is fully compatible with all other Soldered products - just connect and go 😉</InfoBox>
