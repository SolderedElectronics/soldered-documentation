## 3.7V Li-Ion Battery

This is a 3.7V Li-Ion battery with a JST-PH-2mm connector and built-in protection circuitry. It is compatible with Dasduino, Inkplate, and other Soldered boards, and is available in capacities from 40mAh to 10,000mAh.

## Key features

- **Wide capacity range**: 40mAh to 10,000mAh
- **Connector**: JST-PH-2mm (Dasduino and Inkplate compatible)
- **Onboard protection**: overcharge, over-discharge, and short circuit
- **Nominal voltage**: 3.7V
- **Discharge rate**: 1C
