The Inkplate 13SPECTRA can be configured to power on automatically when current is present, bypassing the power switch.

To bypass the power switch, simply short the **[PADS PLACEHOLDER]** pads by directlly adding a small amount of solder, or soldering a 0ohm resistor. The location of these pads is shown below:
