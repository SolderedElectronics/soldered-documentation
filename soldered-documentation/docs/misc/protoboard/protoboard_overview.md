## Protoboard 

**Protoboard** is a simple and versatile board designed for **rapid prototyping and circuit development** without the need for custom PCB fabrication. It provides a grid of **pre-drilled holes with copper pads**, allowing youto easily solder and connect electronic components such as resistors, ICs, sensors and connectors.

Ideal for hobbyists, students and enginners, the protoboard enables quick testing and iteration of circuit designs. Its straightforward layout makes it perfect for building **custom circuits**, **debugging designs** or **creating small standalone electronic projects**. This durable construction ensures reliable connections, while its compact size allows easy integration into larger systems.

The board can be used with a wide range of microcontrollers and development platforms, making it a fundemental tool for electronics development and experimentation.
