## Stereo I2S Audio Amplifier

This digital audio amplifier board uses two **MAX98357A Class D** amplifiers to provide high-quality **stereo audio** output from an **I2S digital audio interface**. This amplifier board delivers **3 watts** of power per channel and can be configured to produce a left channel, right channel or stereo output. Also, it features onboard **gain selection jumpers** (6dB, 9dB, 12dB, or 15dB), allowing you to optimize output levels for different speaker applications. The board can be powered from **3.3V and 5V** and supports sample rates of 8kHz to 96kHz. Typically used in custom music players, IoT devices, alarm systems, etc.

## Key features
- **2 x MAX98357A digital I2S class D amplifiers**
- **Each chip handles one audio channel**
- **3.2W Output Power into 4Ω at 5V**
- **Sample Rates of 8kHz to 96kHz**
- **Onboard jumpers for gain selection**
- **92% Efficiency (RL = 8Ω, POUT = 1W)**
- **Breadboard-compatible design**
