# BMV080 - Overview

## BMV080 Particulate Matter Sensor

The **BMV080** is an ultra-compact **particulate matter (PM)** sensor designed to measure airborne particle concentrations in real time. Its ultra-compact size, low power consumption, and high performance make it an ideal choice for integration into **Internet of Things (IoT)** projects. Its main applications include indoor air quality monitoring, smart home air purifiers, HVAC systems, wearables and portable air monitors.

BMV080 measures mass concentration of airborne particles in the following size categories: **PM1.0** (particles equal to or smaller than 1 µm), **PM2.5** (particles equal to or smaller than 2.5 µm) and **PM10** (particles equal to or smaller than 10 µm). It achieves a typical accuracy of **±10 µg/m³** for lower concentration ranges (between 0 and 100 µg/m³), or **±10% of reading** for higher concentration ranges (from 101 to 1000 µg/m³).

## Key Features
- **Ultra-small form factor**
- **Fan-less design:** no moving parts, uses natural ambient airflow
- **Real-time particulate measurements:** ≈1 reading per second
- **Two measurements modes:** continuous measurement and duty-cycling mode
- **Multiple measurement algorithms:**
  - High precision
  - Balanced
  - Fast response
- **Multi-size particle detection:** measures PM1, PM2.5 and PM10 particle concentration
- **Accuracy:** with ±10 µg/m³ precision up to 100 µg/m³
- **Interface:** simple communication via standard I2C protocol
- **Low-power operation:** configurable duty cycling for battery powered systems
- **Board Dimensions:**  38 x 22mm (1.5 x 0.9 inches)
