## 3-Axis Digital Compass Breakout

The **3-Axis Digital Compass Breakout** is a compact board built around the **AK09918C electronic compass IC**, a highly sensitive **3-axis magnetometer** designed for precise orientation and heading detection. Using advanced **Hall sensor technology**, it accurately measures the Earth’s magnetic field across the **X, Y, and Z axes**, making it ideal for compass applications, navigation systems, robotics, drones, smart wearables, and motion-aware projects.

The sensor provides **16-bit magnetic field measurements** with a sensitivity of **0.15 µT/LSB**, enabling accurate heading and directional sensing. It supports multiple operating modes including **single measurement**, **continuous measurement**, and **self-test mode**, allowing flexible integration into both low-power and real-time applications.

It also includes **2 Qwiic (formerly easyC)** connectors, making it easy to connect with microcontrollers. The Qwiic module connection system eliminates the risk of incorrect connections or damaged modules, ensuring a seamless setup experience.

## Key Features

- **3-axis magnetic field sensing**
  - X, Y, and Z axis terrestrial magnetic field detection
  - Ideal for compass and heading applications
- **Measurement resolution**
  - **16-bit output per axis**
  - **0.15 µT/LSB sensitivity**
- **Measurement range**
  - Up to approximately **±4912 µT**
- **Operating modes**
  - **Power-down mode**
  - **Single measurement mode**
  - **Continuous modes:** 10 Hz / 20 Hz / 50 Hz / 100 Hz
  - **Built-in self-test mode**
- **Power consumption**
  - **Power-down:** 1 µA typical
  - **Measurement:** 1.1 mA typical at 100 Hz
- **Operating voltage**
  - **1.65V to 1.95V**
- **Communication**
  - **I2C interface**
  - Standard mode: **100 kHz**
  - Fast mode: **400 kHz**
  - Fixed I2C address: **0x0C**
- **Additional features**
  - **DRDY (data ready) flag**
  - **overflow detection**
  - **soft reset support**
  - **internal oscillator**
- **Connector**
  - 2 × **Qwiic (formerly easyC) ports**
- **Package dimensions (IC)**
  - **0.8 mm × 0.8 mm × 0.5 mm**
